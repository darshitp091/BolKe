# BolKe: Target Niches & Industries

BolKe is designed to empower the "Bharat" economy. We focus on small-scale industries and local businesses that need an online presence but lack technical skills.

## 🏪 1. Retail & Local Shops (Kirana & General)
- **Kirana Stores:** Daily essentials, home delivery lists.
- **Medical Stores:** Prescription uploads (future), shop timing, location.
- **Stationery & Gift Shops:** Product catalogs, seasonal offers.

## 🥘 2. Food & Beverage (Thelas to Cafes)
- **Street Food Vendors:** Menu display, location tracking, WhatsApp orders.
- **Local Bakeries:** Cake catalogs, custom order forms.
- **Small Cafes/Dhabas:** Digital menus with QR codes.

## 💇 3. Services (Local Professionals)
- **Salons & Barbers:** Service lists, price charts, appointment booking via WhatsApp.
- **Tailors & Boutiques:** Design portfolios, measurement booking.
- **Electricians/Plumbers:** Service area, contact details, "Call Now" buttons.

## 🎨 4. Home-based Businesses (Micro-entrepreneurs)
- **Handmade Crafts:** Jewelry, home decor, customized gifts.
- **Tiffin Services:** Weekly menus, subscription details.
- **Pickle/Masala Makers:** Product variants, bulk order inquiries.

## 🚜 5. Rural & Semi-urban Industries
- **Agri-input Stores:** Seed/Fertilizer availability.
- **Small Workshops:** Fabrication, repair services.
- **Local Artisans:** Traditional crafts, weaving, pottery.

---

## 🎯 Focus Features for these Niches:
1. **Multi-language Support:** Hindi, Marathi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, etc.
2. **WhatsApp-First:** Every action should lead to a WhatsApp conversation.
3. **Image-Heavy:** Simple product photo uploads since "Jo dikhta hai, wo bikta hai."
4. **Offline-to-Online:** Google Maps integration is mandatory for foot traffic.
